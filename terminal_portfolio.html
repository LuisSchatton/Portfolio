About this Portfolio

With this portfolio, I wanted to push myself and my abilities to create something new. Since my passion lies in the field of emerging technologies and design, I wanted to explore the idea of virtual space. As a big fan of Neal Stephensons ‘Snow Crash’, I always wanted to build my own little space on the internet.

The website is built in three.js, without the help of any external plugins. The rudimentary physics engine is completely self-made, with inspiration from the three.js guide and help from my friend (thank you, Yannick!). All 3D assets and most textures are also self-made, mostly ripped from their corresponding project files. The skybox was kindly provided by Jockum Skoglund.

To bring it all together, I made a second Version of this space in Blender and baked the more realistic lighting achievable there to all the textures. This brings greater performance for the Desktop. There are still many things I want to do with this site, but hopefully it was still a fun experience for you!
